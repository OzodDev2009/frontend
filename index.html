Frontend Development

Who Is The Frontend

A front-end developer deals with the preparation of the user-visible part of the website. For example, even for a simple button that you see on websites, a Front-End programmer laboriously writes code. That is, it is the task of the Front-End developer to prepare everything that you see on the screen through the browser, and the collection of these prepared works is called the Front-End part of the website. To put it more simply, the part you see when you visit a website is the Front-End part.
